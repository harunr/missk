About Me

At the age of 24, I can pretty much say I am I am a little ‘bit of everything: From being a nerd that loves books and the science of the mind; to a creative-junky that expresses herself with words; an athlete finding my life-calling in the health and fitness industry, with my love of empowering and inspiring women – combined all into a vision that started 4 years ago.

On a pursuit of self-discovery, whilst helping other people become the best version of themselves, I aim to bring a wave of change to the world.

MY JOURNEY

I started my transformation journey in 2013, during my first year of university. After having realized how much weight I had gained since high school (as I had always struggled with my weight), I decided it was time to make a change. My first weigh-in was set above 80kg, with a body-fat percentage above 38%. However, from the first day, I set my mind on becoming on a Competitive Athlete in the BodyBuilding Industry.

My 2-year journey to stage was one of the most empowering, inspiring, and motivating periods of my life. I developed a passion for health and fitness that branched much further than just stepping on stage. Through social media, I was humbled and inspired to realize how many people I had reached out to via my journey. I believe this is where I found my calling in life – to inspire and motivate other individuals with my passion.

Through my fitness journey and athletic, I aim to inspire, motivate and empower women to set out and achieve their own goals, and be the best possible version of themselves. I want to help women by showing them that anything is possible to achieve, as ling as you have a dream, a vision, and the passion and drive to follow through
